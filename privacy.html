Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

We believe that your personal photos are your business. JigShare is built with a "Privacy by Design" approach. While we have introduced cloud features to enhance your social experience, your photos remain secure, and their lifecycle in the cloud is strictly limited.

Local Processing only

When you select a photo from your library or take a new one, JigShare processes the image locally on your iPhone. We do not upload your photos, grid data, or game progress to any external servers.

  • No Server Uploads: Your images are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Local Storage: Game history and settings are stored locally in your app's sandbox.

Social Sharing & Cloud (24h TTL)

JigShare allows you to share challenges via Universal Links. When you create a challenge, the photo and its metadata are uploaded to our secure cloud. To protect your privacy:

  • 24-Hour Lifecycle: All uploaded challenges are automatically and physically deleted from our servers after 24 hours.
  • Private Sharing: Challenges are point-to-point via generated links. We do not provide a public feed of your content.

Firebase Integration

We use Firebase (a Google service) to provide secure authentication, real-time leaderboards, and notification services (FCM). Your account is managed via Apple Sign-In, which keeps your real identity private while providing a unique User ID (UID) for gameplay.

No Advertising Tracking

JigShare does not use any advertising identifiers or third-party tracking for marketing purposes. We use basic technical identifiers provided by Firebase to maintain your score on leaderboards and deliver challenge notifications.
