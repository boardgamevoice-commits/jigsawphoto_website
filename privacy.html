Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

We believe that your personal photos are your business. Jigsaw Flow is built with a "Privacy by Design" approach, ensuring that your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your device.

Local Processing only

When you select a photo from your library or take a new one, Jigsaw Flow processes the image locally on your iPhone. We do not upload your photos, grid data, or game progress to any external servers.

  • No Server Uploads: Your images are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Local Storage: Game history and settings are stored locally in your app's sandbox.

Social Sharing (.jig files)

Jigsaw Flow allows you to share challenges via ".jig" files. These files contain a compressed version of the photo and the puzzle metadata. When you share this file via iMessage or other apps, you are sending the file directly to your friend. We (the developers) do not have access to these files during or after the transfer.

No Tracking

Jigsaw Flow does not use any tracking SDKs, analytics, or advertising identifiers. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
