2. User Accounts & Firebase

JigShare uses Firebase Auth with Apple Sign-In to manage your account. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account. Your unique User ID (UID) is used to identify you in challenges and on leaderboards.

3. Content Sharing (24h TTL)

When you share a challenge, the photo and metadata are uploaded to our secure cloud storage. To protect your privacy:

  • 24-Hour Lifecycle: All shared challenges and associated data are automatically and physically deleted from our servers after 24 hours.
  • Private Sharing: Challenges are point-to-point. We do not provide a public "square" or feed for your personal photos.
